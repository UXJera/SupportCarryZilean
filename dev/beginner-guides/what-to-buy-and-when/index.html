Optimizing your Items

There are many different schools of thought on how to play the game and itemization, and this item build is what has worked best for me.

As a support, your job is to keep your carries alive and out of danger, provide utility to the team through vision, and be dangerous enough to weild attention from the enemy team.

With that in mind, the best items that have worked for me are items that allow Zilean to spam out his spells without worrying about mana issues, have activatible items to empower your carries, and have mobility/escape to set up wards safely.

There are other builds that want to focus towards damage output, but are not as effective for a support. Later in this guide I discuss some of the "alluring" items that work best for AP support mages, but not for Zilean.

What's the Big Deal about Zeke's Harbinger?

Zeke's Harbinger is an incredibly strong support item that grants 50% critical strike chance AND 20% Ability Power for 5 seconds when fully charged.

Zilean is because he is almost always casting spells with short cooldowns, which means a high uptime for Zeke's Harbinger.

1 charge is gained per second passively, and 4 are gained from an auto attack, and 8 from each spell cast. A common 4-spell combo nets 32 charges in 1 second.

Many of the current AD Carry builds crest at 50% critical chance with Inifinity Edge and Rapid Fire Cannon or Static Shiv. If they have ONLY these two crit items, then keep the Zeke's on them.

If the ADC has more than 50% crit, switch it to an AP Carry. This grants an extra 20% AP, so at 400AP, they get a free Needlessly Large Rod (80AP).

At 600, they get an extra 120 AP.

A full build AP that has 790-820 AP (with flasks) would be pushed close to the 1000 AP mark, making them effectively a 6.5 item carry.

Mikael's Crucible vs. Banner of Command

When Mikael's Crucible is Better
  • When enemy team has multiple sources of CC or long duration CC
  • When money is more of an issue in the game
  • Lanes are pushing AGAINST you
  • Generally, if your team is behind
  • Quicker route to 45% CDR
  • MIKAEL'S DOES NOT STOP SUPPRESSION, ZED ULT, KNOCKUPS, OR BLINDS
When Banner of Command is Better
  • When your team needs extra MR
  • When Jungler didn't buy an Aegis of the Legion item
  • When you have a surplus of gold
  • Lanes are pushing AWAY from you
  • When your team is ahead and has freedom to leave base
  • When 5-man pushing (Banner a Cannon in the side lane)

5-Item Slot Optimized Build

  • Eye of the Oasis
  • Boots of Lucidity
  • Zeke's Harbinger
  • Athene's Unholy Grail
  • Seraph's Embrace
  • Oracle Alteration
  • Vision Wards

This build focuses on 5-slot optimization, while getting 45% CDR and a healthy mana pool.

I do not like this build as much because you are forced to conserve large amounts of gold and use your 6th slot for Vision Wards. In my opinion, pinks are less reliable late game for vision control because there are fewer wards on the map.

Green ward duration is 30 seconds less than previous seasons, and trinket wards last a maximum of 2 minutes, while blue trinket wards last until they are cleared.

This build is useful when going against teams where they use stealth assassins to kill your carry (Rengar, Talon, Kha'Zix, Shaco, Wukong). Since the upgraded red trinket only reveals their location but does not make them right click targetable, it's somewhat ineffective for stopping them.

Alternatively, if you know that the invisible enemy is going to jump on a carry, Zilean can pre-emptively ultimate the carry, so the assassin will have to target you first, and hope that the carry(ies) can finish him off without casualties (but you will probably die if you do this).

The Items to Avoid

  • Spellthief's Edge350g
    • Coin is 48 gpm (4 per creep x 6 creeps x 2 waves) plus 30 life per wave
    • Spellthief is 8 per hit (max of 48 per min) + 10 extra damage (max 60 per min) and 5 AP
    • For Spellthief's to work, you have to put yourself out to harass, making it predictable when your Spellthief is fully stacked.
    • If you don't land Time Bombs or autos, you lose out on gold efficiency
  • Frost Queen's Claim2200g
    • Ancient Coin can be upgraded much later than Frost Queen's, while Frost Queen's demands a high finish priority, thus delaying the rest of your build significantly
    • The active "Spooky Ghosts" seem like a good idea on Zilean, but he can speed himself up and get a stun combo off
    • The active from Talisman is better for engagement/disengage, especially with the Captain Boots enchantment
  • Eye of the Oasis2200g
    • Generally, this is an avoidable buy unless you are going for 5-item slot efficiency (then it's a good buy)
    • No CDR on this item, makes getting 40% CDR from items wonky
    • You gain an extra 50% health/mana regen taking this, but lose the active cooldown and the ability to upgrade into Ruby Sightstone
  • Athene's Unholy Grail2800g
    • You want to buy this ONLY if you are going the 5-item build or if the game goes super lat
    • Athene's gives 20% CDR, which means you give up 2: CDR Boots, Talisman, Zeke's or Mikaels
    • The mana regen from the kill/assist passive isn't that great because you have enough regen full build
  • Morellonomicon2550g
    • Similar theme to Athene's, except offers no defensive stats
    • If someone on the team needs to get the Grievous Wounds buff, it shouldn't be Zilean
    • His damage is high as a support and can't burst through tanks for the debuff
  • Boots of Mobility800g
    • Zilean has insane movespeed with Time Warp, and Movespeed is scalar so you don't benefit as much
    • Dropping down to a base of 350 movespeed is really bad because you can get chain slowed
  • Items built from Needlessly Large Rod3000+ gold
    • Items that build out of Needlessly Large Rod are insanely expensive
    • Inventory space is tight within this build and there isn't space to spare for components
    • AP shouldn't be a focus for Support Zilean, and ultility should be priority 1
    • Archangel's Staff is the ONLY NLR item to go for as a damage buffer with it's active
    • NLR items should only be built on Mid Zilean OR for the final item
