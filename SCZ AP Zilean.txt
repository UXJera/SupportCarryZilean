FABLED AP ZILEAN Pros and Cons

AP Zilean Mid is a strong ally that

Pros:

Can burst out Marksmen and other low HP/low MR targets.
Good wave clear
Can cheat death
Can donate levels (really helps the jungler hit 6 first).

Cons:

Only 1 damage spell, and it's a skillshot.
Falls off when enemies build MR.
Cannot kill tanks. Seriously. This hurts him hard.
