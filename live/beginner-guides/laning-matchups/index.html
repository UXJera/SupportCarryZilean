Laning Matchups

Laning is one of the toughest aspects when learning Zilean support.

Zilean's lane phase is his weakest point in the game, and falling too far behind can ruin your ability to control the game.

The best mindset coming into lane is "Survive the Lane, Win the Game". It is OK to play passive, as long as you aren't dying or losing a large amount of CS.

What does and doesn't lane well with Support Zilean

Zilean has a neutral to passive lane phase, and works well with almost every AD Carry,
specifically if you plan on maxing Time Warp before Time Bomb).

The big advantage that Zilean brings is the incredible mobility and the revive if focused.

Instead of listing every single AD Carry, here are the notable picks that are favorable (or not) with Zilean.

Name

Reasoning behind the rating (More stars means a better lane!)

Vayne

Vayne is my favorite ADC to lane with. She works well with your speed boost and has insane kill potential. Many of Vayne's weaknesses are fixed with Zilean by her side.

Jinx

Jinx has great follow up to CC and can put out high damage. Jinxes are usually dove on, and Zilean offers great dive defense to keep her alive and ticking.

Tristana

Tristana works well with Zilean because the speed boost allows her to safely get more hits while her Explosive Shot is up. Tristana is pretty fragile, but a well-timed Zilean ultimate solves the problem.

Kalista

Kalista works well because the speed boost helps her get more auto attacks in. Both of the two can use ultimates to save each other. This is a pretty solid lane if you play aggressive.

Kog'Maw

Kog'Maw isn't very good right now, but paired with a safe laning phase and protection later in the game, Kog and Zilean work quite well.

Miss Fortune

Miss Fortune WAS very strong, but she's been knocked down a couple of pegs. Her damage is still good, and Zilean can help set up some solid plays.

Lucian

Picking Zilean with Lucian is neutral. The spells that help Lucian are also good for every other champion, but the extra mobility on Lucian makes Zilean a little less worthwhile of a pick.

Graves

Graves is a solid pick overall, but doesn't have the best synergy with Zilean. Zilean helps him get into better positions and land Qs, but it isn't the greatest of lanes.

Ezreal

Of all the AD Carries, Ezreal is the only real bad match with Zilean (the rest are pretty good). Ezreal has good mobility, is very safe, and pokes (rather than all-in). I do not recommend playing Support Zilean if there is an Ezreal on either team.

Enemy Marksmen Matchups

For the most part, the enemy support has a much larger impact on the lane than the AD Carry.

There are some rough matchups for Zilean (those with dashes), and some good matches (those without dashes).

AD Carries that do a massive amount of burst damage are dangerous to Zilean.

Rating
Name

Reasoning behind the rating (More stars means a tougher lane!)

Ezreal

Ezreal is a pain against Zilean because he is so mobile, that landing any CC on him is impossible. His poke has a low cooldown and controlled, so it's hard to time exactly when you need to use your ultimate without wasting it.

Lucian

Lucian does high damage with simple combos and Thunderlords, and is hard to lock down because of his dash. Lucian can punish missed combos easily and is also a common pick in Solo Q.

Draven

A level 5 Draven hits so incredibly hard that you need to be super cautious around that time. Draven can punish so hard and snowballs easily which makes this a very tough lane.

Miss Fortune

Since the nerf to MF's Make it Rain (E), this makes her damage more predictable. She is still popular at the time, but not nearly as strong as she was in pre-season.

Vayne

The same reasons on why Zilean makes Vayne strong are also the same as to why she crumbles. Her mobility gets taken away, her kill target gets revived, and CC for days. An easy lane when using a slow with double bombs.

Kog'Maw

Kog'Maw is highly immobile, and Zilean preys on this. Kog will be forced to burn Flash if he gets slowed, or he dies. You can play very far up against Kog as long as you cycle warding properly.

Enemy Support Matchups

Zilean's greatest threat is the enemy support. There are so many unfavorable matchups that you have to play wisely.

In general, All-In supports and heavy poke supports are strong against Zilean, and tanks are weak.

You should decide if you are maxing Q or E based on the enemy support (not the AD Carry).

Instead of ordering by the difficulty (which changes every patch), here's the list alphabetically.

Alistar

If you slow the Alistar when he tries to engage or leave, he looks like a goof and your ADC can punish him heavily.

Annie

She can be scary, but just be prepared for the level 6 all-in and be ready to ult the lowest ally beforehand. Don't stand next to your AD Carry.

Bard

Bard is annoying with his sustain and poke/cc. When you see him walk away from lane to pick up charms, all-in the enemy ADC because of the 2v1.

Blitzcrank

Blitzcrank is always hard if you can't dodge hooks. A strategy is to double bomb the enemy ADC to reduce the damage yours takes. You can also slow the Blitz if he walks up, then punish him.

Brand

This is my least favorite support to go against. If you don't dodge skillshots, you are history. You can try punishing him for missing spells, but his base damage and 2 second stun are oppressive.

Braum

So his wall can stop bombs from going past him, but auto sticky to him, so you get an easy stun every time. When he walks up, you can slow him and really punish him. He looks like a goof when slowed.

Janna

You'll be forced into a passive lane, which is fine since you want to scale up as well. I don't recommend maxing bombs because her shield mitigates the damage (at a lower mana cost).

Karma

This is a poke champion with shields. She can be quite a nuisance, but you have to play around her mana pool. You can outrun her tether with a speed up on you.

Kennen

Just slow Kennen when he goes in to Lightning Rush and ultimate. Kennen has to be pretty close to be a threat, so you can punish him with CC for coming too close.

Leona

This is the worst matchup for Support Zilean. Your lane doesn't have enough damage to compete with the 2v2, so you HAVE to play passively while her power spikes pass her by.

Lulu

Lulu's kit is similar to Zilean, but with easier poke. This is another tough matchup, because of her poke and CC. You have to be very careful if you try to go for a kill.

Lux

Lux is an annoying poke champ, but nothing special. Her shield is much weaker than your bombs, so it's better to deal more damage than have the better slow.

Morgana

Morgana can shield your bomb combo reliably, but the slow has no cast time. Much like Blitzcrank, if you can dodge her skillshot, you should be fine.

Nami

Nami is the toughest of the sustain lanes. Her poke, CC, and healing are very dangerous. Max Q to make her waste mana on healing, when she's low on mana, that's when you make your move.

Nautilus

Nautilus has fallen out of favor, but he's annoying for a tank with his CC kit. Once Nautilus goes in, it's hard for him to retreat, so you can get heavy damage onto him.

Poppy

Poppy is pretty strong at the moment, just make sure you don't put your back towards a wall or turret. Poppy can ult Zilean, ensuring that the ult isn't casted on a dive target.

Shen

Shen brings good sustain and CC with taunt, but he's punishable when he leaves lane or gets too far ahead of his ADC. You can also double bomb the enemy ADC to prevent some damage.

Sona

Sona's poke and sustain hurt, so just kill her quick with an all-in. She's an easy kill with levels in Q at levels 2, 4 and 5 if she's out of position.

Soraka

Soraka is commonly banned, but she isn't too much of a problem for Zilean. If you can land damage onto her, it works out well. If Soraka dies or isn't in lane, you are winning.

Tahm Kench

Tahm Kench is one of thse supports that you don't expect to make it past bans. This guy is a huge pain, as usual. You have to play passive against him, because he can eat the damage from double bombs.

Taric

Taric is getting reworked soon, but for the present time, he's not a tough match up. His mana pool is very low, and you don't need strong double bombs to get it low.

Thresh

This is a common matchup into Zilean. Dodge hooks and double bomb the ADC is the strategy. This matchup is hard while you're learning it, but gets easier with practice.

Trundle

All you have to do against Trundle is slow him when he tries to pillar you off. He has good sustain, so you can't really poke him out, so you have to all-in to get the kill.

Vel'Koz

Vel'Koz is similar to Brand, but it takes many more skillshots to kill you. Landing slows on him is difficult, but he is fragile and strong double bombs along with ADC follow up make for easy kills.

Zyra

Zyra is a tough damage lane with CC. Her damage is similar to Karma, but without the sustain. Zyra's plants can make bombs target them, but Zyra herself is fragile.
